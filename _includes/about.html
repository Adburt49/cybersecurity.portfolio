About Me

Cybersecurity Analyst

Cybersecurity trailblazer with a passion for safeguarding digital landscapes. Pursuing a Google Cybersecurity Professional Certificate and equipped with a solid foundation in healthcare, I blend compassion and tech-savviness to create a unique professional profile.

My journey began as a curious teenager fascinated by coding and Linux. After a fulfilling stint as a home health aide and as a mother of two, I returned to tech with renewed focus and drive. I’ve honed my attention to detail, problem-solving abilities, and time management skills.

"From safeguarding patient care to protecting digital landscapes—it's all about trust, resilience, and vigilance."

Managing patient care required strict adherence to privacy and compliance standards, seamlessly translating to maintaining data confidentiality and regulatory compliance in cybersecurity.

Throughout my career, I’ve conducted risk assessments and implemented preventative measures, which are vital in identifying and mitigating threats in digital environments. My ability to stay calm under pressure and communicate effectively has proven invaluable in emergencies and team collaborations.

Armed with these skills, I’m ready to contribute to the cybersecurity field by safeguarding digital landscapes, uncovering vulnerabilities, and creating robust defenses. Whether it’s securing networks, analyzing malware, or educating users, I’m committed to making a difference.

I recently completed my training with Merit America, involving 5 months of intensive professional development, the Google Cybersecurity Professional Certificate, and several hands-on technical projects. Together, let's create secure and resilient digital environments.
